Zatar Chicken Sneakers

INGREDIENTS

1 ½ tablespoons za’atar seasoning chicken tenders 4 tablespoons, olive oil,2 cups packed spinach leaves ½ cup packed mint leaves ,1 clove garlic,1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice

STEPS

Step 1 Sprinkle za’atar seasoning over both sides of chicken tenders. Step 2 Broil or grill chicken for 7 minutes on each side or until no longer pink in center. Step 3 Place spinach, mint, jalapeño, garlic, lemon juice and bouillon in food processor; cover.
